Lugage & Tracking system

A luggage tracking system is a technology that enables travelers to track the whereabouts of their luggage during transit. These systems use various technologies such as RFID, GPS, or Bluetooth to track the location of luggage, and can provide real-time updates to travelers through mobile applications or other communication channels.!
